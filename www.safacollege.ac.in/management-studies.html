The Department of Management Studies was established in the 2009–2010 academic year with the aim of promoting high-quality management education. The department provides a three-year, six-semester Bachelor of Business Administration program with a finance concentration. In order to achieve corporate administration goals, the department offers programs on the newest ideas, methods, instruments, and strategies.
