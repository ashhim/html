The Department of Languages was established in 2008 to offer our college's undergraduate students required language courses. Hindi, Arabic, and Malayalam are the common supplemental courses offered by the department. Five committed faculty members teach Hindi, Arabic, and Malayalam in our team. Our goals are to raise multilingualism among college students and improve their language proficiency.
