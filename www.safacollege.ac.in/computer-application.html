Bachelor of Computer Applications (BCA) Program was introduced in Safa college of arts and science in the academic year 2010. The department provides a prominent  academic ambience with qualified core faculty members and fully fledged computer lab to develop domain knowledge and rational skills of students. The Program includes the study of software Engineering, Operating Systems, Computer Networks, Web Technology, Android Application Development, Advanced Java, RDBMS, DataStructures etc.,. Highly interactive Seminars and guest lectures with Industry-oriented resource persons are conducted to help the students to bridge the gap between education and industrial need.
