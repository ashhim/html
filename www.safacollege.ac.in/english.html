  • STUDENT PROGRESSION
The English department was established as a general department when Safa College of Arts and Science first opened its doors in 2009 with the intention of providing a higher education in English language and literature that was based on moral principles. The MA program in English language and literature was added in 2018, upgrading to postgraduate department. .A variety of teaching and learning resources ,including ICT enabled classrooms are available in the department, helping to foster a positive learning environment and guarantee an engaging educational experience. We provide intensive mentoring to our students both in academicand extra curricular activities. Our program has also been providing Gender studies, writing for the media, Translations in world classics etc. The department aims to educate the students about the contemporary forms of culture through English literature.
